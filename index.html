ABOUT ME

I am a passionate software engineer who has more than 9 years of hands-on development and management experience. I worked for both large scale and medium scale companies also founded a startup.

I’ve developed and designed web-based products and both online/offline systems. I’ve experienced on OOP based languages and SQL, also system side configuration and coding. I’ve worked with cloud solutions such as AWS, Google Cloud, Azure. For the last 2 years in my career, I worked as a Software Architect who makes software design and technology stack choices, dictates software coding standards and leads the development team. I am involved in all aspects of the development process. Right now, I am continuing my work as a Software Architect in a healthcare company.
